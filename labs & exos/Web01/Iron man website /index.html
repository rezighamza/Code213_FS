About The Armored Avenger

Anthony "Tony" Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor, suffers a severe chest injury during a kidnapping. His captors attempt to force him to build a weapon of mass destruction, but he instead creates a powered suit of armor to save his life and escape captivity. Later, Stark develops his suit, adding weapons and other technological devices he designed through his company, Stark Industries.
